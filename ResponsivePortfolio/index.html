About Me

Originally from Indianapolis, I relocated to Seattle in 2017 in search of new growth opportunities. I currently work in hospitality and run the bar program for a restaurant in Wallingford.


I studied Public Relations and Marketing at the University of Indianapolis and held several positions in fundrasing and staff training and development. I recently took an interest in learning a new career path, which led me to the University of Washington Coding Bootcamp in December of 2020.


When I'm not mixing drinks and studying coding, you can usually find me traveling (pre-pandemic anyways), rearranging my houseplants, reading, hiking in the Enchantments, kayaking or baking pies
